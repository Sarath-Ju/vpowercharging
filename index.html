TYPES OF EV CHARGERS

There are two types of EV chargers based on type of current. The AC charges are comparatively slow chargers and DC has a range of fast charging capabilities.

FAST CHARGERS

These are fast chargers for any EV and are usually positioned along major roads throughout the country. The devices deliver high-power direct current (DC) to quickly recharge an EV. This machine capacity generally ranges from 15 kw to 150 kW. However, the fast charging also depends upon the capacity of the EV to take charge in a specified time.

SLOW CHARGERS

The AC chargers provide slow charging for the EVs. These slow chargers require less power and hence can be installed at any domestic complexes easily.
